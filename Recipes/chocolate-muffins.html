Moist Chocolate Muffins

Tasty moist chocolate muffins that will keep you coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C. Line 12 muffin cups with muffin liners.
  3. Combine flour, sugar, 3/4 cup chocolate chips, cocoa powder and baking soda in a large bowl.
  4. Whisk yogurt, milk, oil, egg and vannilla in a separate bowl until smooth.
  5. Pour yogurt mixture into chocolate mixture and stir until batter is just blended.
  6. Fill prepaired muffin cups 3/4 full and sprinle with remaining 1/4 cup chocolate chips.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven, about 20 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
